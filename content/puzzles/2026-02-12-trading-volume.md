Why is there this *immense* volume of trading? When was the last time you bought or sold a stock? You don't do it every 20 milliseconds, do you?

If I get my list of the 10 great unsolved puzzles that I hope our grandchildren will have figured out, why does getting the information into asset prices require that the stock be turned over a hundred times?
